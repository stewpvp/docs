> Create, manage, and interact with player guilds on the server.

## Configuring guilds

### Creating a guild

This command is used to create a new guild with a unique name and a short chat tag.

<Warning>
  Guild names and tags must be unique across the server. If the name or tag is already taken, the creation will fail. The guild **name** and **tag** will be visible to all players in chat.
</Warning>

<CodeGroup>

```javascript Syntax
/g create (name) (tag)
```

```javascript Example
/g create Nightfall NF
```

</CodeGroup>

<br />

### Managing members

These commands allow you to kick, promote, demote, and transfer leadership within your guild.

<Info>
  The `player` argument is the username of the guild member you want to target. Use `/g kick` to remove them, `/g promote` or `/g demote` to change their rank, or `/g transfer` to hand over guild leadership.
</Info>

<CodeGroup>

```javascript Syntax
/g kick (player)
/g promote (player)
/g demote (player)
/g transfer (player)
```

```javascript Example
/g kick Steve
/g promote Alex
```

</CodeGroup>

<br />

### Guild communication

You can send messages exclusively to your guild using guild chat, or broadcast a callout to all online players with your guild tag.

<Info>
  Use the `/g chat` command to toggle guild-only chat. Use `/g callout` to send a server-wide announcement branded with your guild tag.
</Info>

<CodeGroup>

```javascript Syntax
/g chat
/g callout (message)
```

```javascript Example
/g callout We are recruiting! Join Nightfall today.
```

</CodeGroup>

## Tips for guild leaders

<Steps>
  <Step title="Recruiting members">
    To grow your guild, invite players with `/g invite` and use `/g callout` to broadcast a recruitment message to all online players. Players can also join your guild themselves with `/g join` or leave with `/g leave`.

    <Info>
      The `player` argument must be a currently online player. Use `/g who
      (player)` to check if a player already belongs to another guild before inviting them.
    </Info>

    <CodeGroup>

    ```javascript Syntax
    /g invite (player)
    /g callout (message)
    ```

    ```javascript Example
    /g invite Steve // Invites Steve to your guild
    /g callout We are recruiting! // Broadcasts to all online players
    ```

    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Maintaining your guild">
    Regularly review your roster with `/g info (guild)` and remove inactive members with `/g kick`. Use `/g promote` to give trusted members officer status so they can help manage invites and keep the guild active alongside you.
  </Step>
</Steps>
