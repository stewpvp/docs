## How to obtain Donor Perks

Donor perks can be purchased via **store packages** in our [**Support Server**](https://discord.gg/stew) which can be found at the top of the channel list labeled as **Server Store**.

<Warning>
  **iOS** and **Android** devices will be subject to pay extra due to the **App Store** or **Google Play** fees.
</Warning>
